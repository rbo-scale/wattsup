How to play

☀️Solar panels generate free power during the day. Clouds and storms reduce output.
🔋Charge the battery when you have surplus power. Discharge it when demand spikes.
⚙️The gas generator is reliable but costs money every second it runs. Use it as backup.
🍬The factory earns candy money every second it runs. Keep supply ≥ demand!
💥3 blackouts and the candy business closes. Don't let the lights go out!
